October 2025

ðŸ‡¯ðŸ‡µ 3 amazing weeks in Japan. For Kay and I, October was our big break month in 2025. We ventured to Japan, partly in order to meet up with my sister and her husband who were traveling there at around the same time. We met them for a day in Kyoto squeezed in between spending time in Hiroshima, Kurashiki, Kinosaki Onsen, Matsue, and Izumo.

It was a wonderful three weeks, visiting a few spots for the first time while also exploring some places we'd already visited a little deeper.

A definite highlight were the 6 days we spent in Matsue and Izumo, a part of Japan that neither Kay or I had been near before. Matsue in particular was such a chill city, with things operating at a pleasant pace, and some beautiful spots along the lakeside to spend the afternoon. We happened to be there in time to catch the end of the city's lantern festival around Matsue Castle, which included a really nice little open air night market.

I'd love to explore that region a bit more if time allows ~~
