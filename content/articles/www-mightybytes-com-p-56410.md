Learn how to create and implement effective yet flexible communications strategies for uncertain times in this educational webinar series and toolkit.

Technological disruption and political uncertainty wreak havoc on impactful communications strategies. To say communications leaders currently work in a complicated landscape is an understatement. Increasing democratic uncertainty has organizations and teams on edge. Plus, the firehose of AI-generated content undermines campaign effectiveness and sows disinformation. 

If you have specific marketing or advocacy benchmarks to reach, this political and technological disruption adversely impacts your organization’s ability to remain viable. Navigating Chaos: Communications Strategies for Uncertain Times is a six-part webinar series to help responsible communications leaders and their stakeholders connect and thrive during disruptive, unpredictable events.

If you need to create a meaningful plan of action that drives impactful marketing or advocacy efforts throughout 2025, this series can help. Seasoned experts will share lessons learned from effectively balancing growth and impact during times of chaos.

The practical ideas and insights shared in this series will help you lead your organization through uncertain times. Learn more about each session in the individual webinar descriptions below.

The first webinar in this series focused on the foundations of impactful storytelling.

1. Foundations of Impactful Storytelling

Neuroscience has proven that strong storytelling is a must-have communications tool. During this webinar, we covered some key foundational storytelling techniques you can use to build relationships and inspire action. We also tied these techniques to proven strategies for impactful communications. 

You will learn:

Why good storytelling is essential for an effective communications strategy.

Science-based storytelling techniques that inspire action.

How to use these techniques in your own communications or marketing efforts.

The second webinar in our series focused on how to foster resilient communications strategies to account for unpredictability.

2. Resilient Communications Strategies

When external factors disrupt our best laid communications plans, chaos might not be far behind. This webinar explores how to craft inclusive, accessible, and more sustainable communications strategies that are resilient enough to withstand disruption yet effective enough to ensure your marketing or advocacy efforts reach their targets. 

Attendees will learn:

How to nurture abundance and a growth mindset during times of chaos and scarcity.

How to co-create an effective message architecture to drive successful communications.

Tactics for an impactful content strategy in the age of ‘enshittification’.

3. Coalition- & Community-Building

Communities and coalitions grounded in shared values can create collective action. However, in times of chaos, it’s easy for these efforts to go off the rails. In this conversation, we discuss how coalition- and community-building creates opportunities to go further together and how collaboration, co-creation, and resilience strategies can help you maintain consistency over time. 

You will learn:

The value of collaboration, co-creation, and facilitation in designing effective strategies.

The role of scale, reciprocity, and personal investment in community-building. 

How to tie coalition- and community-building efforts with impactful content and communications strategies.
